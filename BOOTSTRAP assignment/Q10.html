Historical Monuments of India

Taj Mahal

Built by Emperor Shah Jahan in memory of his wife Mumtaz Mahal, the Taj Mahal is an epitome of Mughal architecture.

Red Fort

The Red Fort in Delhi, made of red sandstone, was the main residence of the emperors of the Mughal dynasty for nearly 200 years.

Gateway of India

The Gateway of India in Mumbai was built in 1924 to commemorate the visit of King George V and Queen Mary to India.

Qutub Minar

The Qutub Minar in Delhi is a UNESCO World Heritage Site, known for its 73-meter tall minaret and intricate carvings.
